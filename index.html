Sicilians in Monterey's Canning Industries

The Sicilian immigrants made valuable contributions to the growth and success of the canning industry in Monterey, helping to establish the city as one of the most important canning centers in the United States.

However, their exclusive communities and opposition to unionization had far-reaching effects on other immigrant groups, leading to tensions between different races and making it difficult for other workers to improve their conditions.
